The Conflict

The Clone Wars (22–19 BBY) was a galaxy-spanning conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Orchestrated by the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, the war was designed to destroy the Jedi Order and pave the way for the Galactic Empire.

"Begun, the Clone War has."
— Grand Master Yoda

Leading the charge are the Jedi Knights, commanding vast armies of clone troopers bred on the planet Kamino. Against them stand the Droid Armies of the Separatists, relentless and infinite in number.

Important Events

22 BBY

Battle of Geonosis

The spark that ignited the war. The Jedi Strike Team intervenes to rescue Obi-Wan Kenobi, revealing the massive Clone Army for the first time.

22 BBY

Ashoka assigned as Anakin's Padawan

Ahsoka Tano is officially assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan, marking a significant moment in the Jedi Order's history.

21 BBY

Second Battle of Geonosis

After finding about new droid fabrics, the Republic launches a full-scale assault on Geonosis. Everything is falling apart, from the beginning. All Jedi misses thier landing zones, failing to meet in randezvous point. Even though, they manage to win the battle, it was a costly victory.

21 BBY

Battle of Kamino

The Separatists launch a surprise attack on Kamino, the birthplace of the clone troopers. The Republic's forces are caught off guard, by the unexpected assault of aqua droids coming out from the ocean. Assaj Ventress tries to steel the clone host's - Jango Fet's DNA template, but is ultimately foiled by Jedi Master Anaking Skywalker and his troopers. Despite heavy losses, the Republic manages to defend Kamino and secure the future of the Clone Army.

20 BBY

Count Dooku betrays Assaj Ventress

Count Dooku betrays Assaj Ventress, revealing his true allegiance to the Separatist cause. This betrayal marks a turning point in the war, as Ventress is forced to flee and seek refuge between Nighsisters, that are eventually wiped out by Dooku's orders after assasination attempt on him.

21 BBY

Battle of Umbara

The Republic launches a campaign to take Umbara from Separatists grasp, as it was strategic point to controll important hyperspace routes, especially one's leading to Kashyyyk. The battle is marked by dense, dark atmosphere on the shadowy planet and the controversial leadership of Jedi General Pong Krell, whose ruthless tactics lead to significant clone casualties and eventual mutiny as he turned out to be a traitor working for the Separatists.

20 BBY

Siege of Mandalore

A joint operation led by Ahsoka Tano to liberate Mandalore from Maul's rule. One of the final battles before the fall of the Republic.

19 BBY

Order 66

The ultimate betrayal. Clone troopers turn against their Jedi Generals, marking the end of the war and the rise of the Empire.
